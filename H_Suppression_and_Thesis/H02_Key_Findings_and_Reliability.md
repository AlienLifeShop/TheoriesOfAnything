Scientific methodology demands reproducible evidence and controlled analysis.
- Critics have noted that extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence, and the current evidentiary basis remains insufficient by conventional academic standards.

### Alternative Explanations & Disputed Evidence

- **Alternative explanations:** Conventional frameworks offer well-documented explanations for many phenomena associated with Key Findings and Reliability Assessment. Critics argue these conventional explanations should be exhausted before more speculative interpretations are pursued.
