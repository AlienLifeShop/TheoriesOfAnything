## PLACEMENT RULES

1. If a topic spans two sections → place in the section where its **PRIMARY evidence type** lives. Cross-reference the other.
2. Never duplicate full content across sections → use cross-references instead.
3. **H section is meta-only** → analysis ABOUT the research, not research itself.
4. Modern science topics go in **G** if they CONNECT to ancient knowledge. Pure science goes in **Q** or **R**.
5. When in doubt, ask: "What TYPE of evidence is this primarily about?" That's your section.
