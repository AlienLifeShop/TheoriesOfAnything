### 2. WHY DO AI SYSTEMS HALLUCINATE — AND IS IT FIXABLE?

**Domain:** Artificial Intelligence & Epistemology
**Status:** Architecturally unresolved
**Tier:** 1 (the phenomenon is verified; the solution is not)

Large Language Models confidently generate plausible but factually wrong statements because they predict *likely text* rather than *verified facts*. No architecture-level fix exists — mitigations (RAG, chain-of-thought verification, grounding) are partial. The deeper question: **is hallucination eliminable, or is it inherent to any system that generates language statistically?** This connects directly to the philosophical question of whether statistical pattern-matching can ever constitute "understanding" (**Searle's** Chinese Room argument, 1980). If LLMs don't *understand* — if syntax ≠ semantics — then hallucination isn't a bug but a fundamental feature of the architecture.

**The puzzle:** We don't know whether "understanding" is even a meaningful category distinct from sufficiently sophisticated pattern prediction.

> **🔬 BEST-FIT THEORY: Hallucination is an inherent architectural feature** of next-token prediction, not a fixable bug. LLMs are "stochastic parrots" (**Bender & Gebru**, 2021) that model *linguistic probability*, not *truth*. Supported by ZD_2_12 (claims of LLM "understanding" tagged **[DEBUNKED]**), S_1_11, K_3_01. **Searle's** Chinese Room and **Shanahan's** role-play framing both predict hallucination as a necessary consequence of syntax without semantics. Mitigations (RAG, tool-use, chain-of-thought) reduce frequency but cannot eliminate the root cause without a fundamentally different architecture. *This is a corpus-grounded inference, not established fact.*

**The puzzle:** The most confirmed objects in astrophysics (black holes) contain the most extreme breakdown of physics (singularities), and their time-reverse (white holes) may have created the universe.

### 4. WHAT IS DARK MATTER — AND WHAT IS DARK ENERGY?

**Domain:** Cosmology & Particle Physics
**Status:** 95% of the universe is unknown
**Tier:** 1 (existence confirmed by 6+ independent lines of evidence; identity unknown)

Dark matter (27% of the universe) has six independent confirmations — galaxy rotation curves, gravitational lensing, CMB anisotropy, galaxy cluster dynamics, large-scale structure, Bullet Cluster — yet after 40+ years of searching, **no particle has been detected**. WIMPs, axions, primordial black holes, sterile neutrinos — all remain candidates. Dark energy (68% of the universe) is even more mysterious: the cosmological constant problem (QFT prediction off by 10^120) is "the worst prediction in physics history." DESI 2024 data hints dark energy may be *dynamic* (changing over time), not constant — potentially revolutionary. And the **Hubble tension** (local H₀ ≈ 73 km/s/Mpc vs. CMB H₀ ≈ 67.4 km/s/Mpc, a ~5σ discrepancy confirmed by JWST) suggests we may need entirely new physics.

**The puzzle:** We can see only 5% of what the universe is made of.

> **🔬 BEST-FIT THEORY: Real particles (axions favored over WIMPs) + dynamic dark energy.** The Bullet Cluster provides near-definitive evidence that dark matter is *real particles*, not modified gravity. Axions have overtaken WIMPs as the leading candidate after decades of null WIMP results. For dark energy, DESI 2024 data hints it may be *evolving* over cosmic time, not a fixed cosmological constant — potentially revolutionary. The Hubble tension (~5σ) may require entirely new physics beyond ΛCDM. *This is a corpus-grounded inference, not established fact.*

Meanwhile, congenitally blind patients report detailed visual perception during NDEs.

**The puzzle:** Neither the production model (brain generates consciousness) nor the filter model (brain receives consciousness) is proven. The evidence is tantalizing but not conclusive.

This is the only theory in the corpus that accounts for ALL four major anomalies simultaneously: (1) NDE consciousness during verified flat-line EEG, (2) psilocybin *reducing* brain activity while *increasing* subjective experience, (3) terminal lucidity in patients with destroyed brains, (4) Borjigin's post-mortem gamma surges exceeding waking levels. The production model must explain each of these as separate anomalies; the filter model predicts them all. *This is a corpus-grounded inference, not established fact.*

Life appeared within 400–600 Myr of Earth’s formation, suggesting abiogenesis may be easy given the right conditions. Dirac's equation predicting antimatter and Riemann geometry pre-fitting spacetime curvature are corpus-highlighted examples. **Status:** The Page curve is derived but the physical mechanism is unknown
**Tier:** 2

**The puzzle:** The most fundamental laws of physics contradict each other inside black holes, and resolving this contradiction may reveal how spacetime itself works.

> **🔬 BEST-FIT THEORY: Island Formula + ER=EPR** — information IS preserved; **Hawking** conceded in 2004. The island formula (**Penington** 2019; **Almheiri et al.** 2019–2020) derived the Page curve from semiclassical gravity using "replica wormholes." Information loss is now classified **Tier 4 (REJECTED)** in ZA_2_12. AdS/CFT is "manifestly unitary." The physical mechanism remains unknown, but the theoretical consensus has shifted decisively: black holes are information scramblers, not information destroyers. ER=EPR connects the resolution to emergent spacetime (Q19). *This is a corpus-grounded inference, not established fact.*
