| Tier | Meaning |
|------|---------|

| **TIER 2** | Credible — debated dating or classification but physically real |

## PURPOSE

This is a **comprehensive geographic catalog** of every known pyramid, pyramid-like structure, and pyramidal monument on Earth, organized by continent and region, with GPS coordinates, dimensions, dates, and builder identity where known. D02 provides analysis and theory — this document is the **complete location database**.

### Proposed "Great Circle" Alignments

Sites along a proposed great circle alignment (~30° inclination to equator):
- Giza → Petra → Mohenjo-daro → Angkor Wat → Easter Island → Nazca → (return to Giza)

**Mainstream View:** Given enough ancient sites, statistically significant alignments will appear by chance. This is a documented cognitive bias (patternicity).

**Alternative View:** Too many major sites cluster near specific latitudes and alignments to be coincidental. Ancient builders understood/used global geodesy.
